FAQs

Common questions about bathroom renovations

Can I stay in the property during the renovation? ↓
Yes, most clients stay in their home during bathroom work. We work to minimise disruption—protecting floors, containing dust, and ensuring you have access to sanitary facilities throughout.
How long does a typical bathroom renovation take? ↓
A standard bathroom renovation typically takes 2–4 weeks, depending on scope, materials and whether structural or layout changes are involved. We'll confirm the timeline in your detailed quote.
Do you supply materials and sanitaryware? ↓
Yes, we can supply through our trade accounts. Alternatively, we can work with materials and sanitaryware you've chosen yourself. Both routes are included in our quote options.
What's included in your quote? ↓
Our quotes are broken down by scope—labour, materials, and any specialist elements are listed separately so you can see exactly where the cost sits and make informed decisions.
Do you offer a guarantee on bathroom work? ↓
Yes—we offer a 12-month workmanship guarantee on all bathroom renovation work, covering materials and labour for defects arising from our installation.

Project scope

What we delivered

A full programme delivered over 10 weeks, managing all trades in-house with weekly client updates and a structured milestone plan.

1
Ground-floor layout reconfiguration

Removal of structural wall between kitchen and rear reception to create a single open-plan living and kitchen space with RSJ steel installation.

2
Full kitchen installation (Howdens)

Supply-and-install via our Howdens trade account — shaker-style units, quartz worktops, integrated appliances and under-cabinet lighting.

3
Bathroom renovation

Full strip-out and replacement with walk-in shower, freestanding bath, large-format wall tiles and underfloor heating.

4
Full electrical rewire (Part P certified)

Complete rewire of ground and first floors, new consumer unit, smart lighting to kitchen and living areas.

5
Flooring throughout

Engineered oak flooring to ground floor. Encaustic-effect porcelain tiles to bathroom and WC.

6
Decorating & carpentry

Full replastering, skimming and painting throughout. Bespoke alcove shelving and period-appropriate skirting and architrave.
